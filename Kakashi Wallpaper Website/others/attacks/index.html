Attacks

Chidori

Chidori is an A-ranked Jutsu developed by Kakashi Hatake.Literally meaning One Thousand Birds, this technique makes use of lightning style chakra in the palm of the user to create a powerful thrust-based technique

Sharingan

Kakashi was a Sharingan user for the majority of his ninja life. His prowess with the eye was said to be on par with that of an Uchiha.Using this eye, Kakashi was able to cast a powerful genjutsu on his opponents.

Kamui

Kamui is the Mangekyo Sharingan ability of Obito Uchiha. Kakashi, having obtained one Sharingan eye from Obito. When used Kakashi gained the ability to warp anything within a short range from him to the dimension of Kamui.

Susanoo

After gaining access to both Mangekyo Sharingan during the Fourth Great Ninja War, Kakashi Hatake managed to awaken the powers of the Susanoo.This Susanoo was stronger than others susanoo because it had the powers of Kamui.

Rasengan

Kakashi Hatake is one of the very few users of the Rasengan technique. Although he doesn't use it much, Kakashi mastered this Jutsu at quite a young age.

Raikiri

Raikiri, nicknamed Lightning Cutter, is the signature technique of Kakashi Hatake and a better version of the Chidori. Like the Chidori, the Raikiri is a thrust-based technique that relies on speed and penetration power to end the enemy instantly.

Shiden

Shiden, also known as Purple Electricity, is one of the strongest and newest techniques of Kakashi Hatake. After losing his Sharingan during the Fourth Great Ninja War, Kakashi could no longer rely on the Raikiri or the Chidori.

Kamui Raikiri

Kamui Raikiri is the strongest known Jutsu of Kakashi Hatake. Combining the powers of Kamui with that of a Raikiri enhanced with Six Paths chakra.
